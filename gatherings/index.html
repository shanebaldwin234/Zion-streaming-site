§ 02

What a gathering is.

And what it isn't
Four
rhythms.

Teaching. Several long-form teaching sessions across the weekend — the kind of depth that does not fit inside an episode. Bring a notebook.

Music. Live worship and original music in the evenings. Often spontaneous. Always the highlight.

Fire. Real fires in the evening. Real conversation. Real testimony. The thing nobody can program on a stage.

Community. Meals together. Walks together. Hard questions asked in person. People leave with friends they did not have when they arrived.
