About
the work.

Zion Streaming is the publishing home of Zion Media — a pro–Latter-day Saint studio building media for a moment most of the world is sleeping through. Three hosts. A growing remnant. One commission: gather Israel one by one.

§ 01 — Origin How it
began.

Zion Media was started by three men who wanted to talk about scripture the way it deserves to be talked about — without the carefully managed safety of a Sunday lesson and without the cynicism of the internet. Bold, careful, reverent, and unafraid.

What began as a YouTube channel has grown into a studio: three podcast lines, original music, a community gathered in person several times a year, and a coming slate of full-length documentary work.

The premise is simple. The restoration of all things is not finished. Scripture has more to say than has been said. There is a remnant being prepared. We make the work for that remnant.

§ 03

What we hold.

A short statement
Four
tenets.

1. The Restoration is not finished. The work of the Restoration of all things is ongoing. Scripture, history, and revelation continue to unfold for those who study them.

2. Hard questions are sacred. Truth survives scrutiny. We do not flinch from difficult passages, contested doctrines, or the gaps that careful study reveals.

3. Gathering is a real thing. The gathering of Israel is not a metaphor and not a marketing strategy. It happens one person, one family, one covenant at a time.

4. Light over noise. We make media that holds up — not content that performs. Every episode, every song, every gathering is built to outlast the algorithm.
